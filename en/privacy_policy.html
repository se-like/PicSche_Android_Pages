Privacy Policy

PicSche (the "App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used and protected.

1. Information We Collect

The App accesses the following information:

(1) Camera and Photo Library
Purpose: To select or capture images containing schedule information (e.g. school handouts, event flyers)
Storage: Images are deleted immediately after schedule extraction and are not stored within the App

(2) Calendar
Purpose: To add extracted schedule information to your device's calendar
Storage: Schedule data is saved to your device's default calendar app and is not stored on any server operated by the App

(3) Text in Images
Purpose: To extract schedule information (title, date/time, location, etc.) via AI analysis
Storage: Extracted schedule data is held temporarily in memory and is deleted after it is added to the calendar

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses external AI analysis services to accurately extract schedule information from images.

(1) Information Sent

  • Images you select or capture
  • Instructions for image analysis

(2) Purpose
To automatically extract schedule information (title, date/time, location, notes) using AI

(3) AI Services Used

The App may use the following AI services:

  • Anthropic Claude API (https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
  • Other AI services capable of accurate image analysis

Services used may be changed without notice for improvement of the App.

(4) Data Handling

  • Images are processed according to each AI service provider's policy after analysis
  • The Claude API (Anthropic) used by the App does not use data sent via the API for model training under its published policy. For details, see each provider's privacy policy

(5) Protection of Personal Information
The App only asks the AI to extract schedule fields (title, date/time, location, notes) and does not request extraction of name, address, phone number, or other dedicated personal-identification fields. However, text visible in the image may appear in titles or notes, and we cannot guarantee complete prevention.

2-2. Advertising (AdMob)

The App displays banner and reward ads via Google's AdMob service when you use the free plan.

(1) Information collected or transmitted

AdMob (Google LLC) may collect the following for ad delivery:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (model, OS version, etc.)
  • IP address
  • Other information as described in Google's Privacy Policy

(2) Purpose

Optimization of ad delivery and display; detection of fraud; etc.

(3) Third-party policies

For details on how AdMob handles data, see Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

(4) Additional collection by the App

The App does not transmit personally identifiable information (name, email address, etc.) to AdMob for advertising purposes.

3. Use of Information

Collected information is used only for:

  • Extracting schedule information from images
  • Adding extracted events to the calendar
  • Improving the App and fixing issues

The App does not use information for:

  • Additional data collection by the App for advertising (Note: AdMob may collect device information etc. for ad display. See "2-2. Advertising (AdMob)" above)
  • Tracking user behavior (except for analytics and improvement)
  • Selling or providing data to third parties (except as required for AI analysis API and advertising services)

4. Data Retention

  • Image data: Deleted immediately after analysis (retention: seconds)
  • Extracted schedule data: Deleted from the App after calendar registration
  • Events added to the calendar: Stored in your device's calendar app until you delete them

The App does not store user data on any server.

5. Data Sharing

The App does not share your information with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • When you have given consent
  • As described in "Third-Party Services" and "2-2. Advertising (AdMob)" above

6. Security

The App protects your information by:

  • Keeping images only temporarily (no permanent storage)
  • Using encrypted API communication (HTTPS)
  • Minimizing data kept in the App

The Claude API (Anthropic) used by the App does not use data sent via the API for model training under its published policy.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke camera, photo, or calendar access (via Android Settings)
  • Uninstall the App (all App data will be removed)
  • Delete events added to the calendar (via your device's calendar app)

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. If a child under 13 uses the App, they should do so with parental consent and supervision.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated without notice due to legal changes or App updates. Significant changes will be communicated in the App.
