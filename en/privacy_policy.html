Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

PicSche (the "App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used and protected.

1. Information We Collect

The App accesses the following information:

(1) Camera and Photo Library
Purpose: To select or capture images containing schedule information (e.g. school handouts, event flyers)
Storage: Images are deleted immediately after schedule extraction and are not stored within the App

(2) Calendar
Purpose: To add extracted schedule information to your device's calendar
Storage: Schedule data is saved to your device's default calendar app and is not stored on any server operated by the App

(3) Text in Images
Purpose: To extract schedule information (title, date/time, location, etc.) via AI analysis
Storage: Extracted schedule data is held temporarily in memory and is deleted after it is added to the calendar

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses external AI analysis services to accurately extract schedule information from images.

(1) Information Sent

  • Images you select or capture
  • Instructions for image analysis (including text you enter in the "image context" field)
  • For the free plan, we manage usage counts on our server. A device-derived identifier (based on Android ID; it does not identify individuals) is sent to our API, and usage count, reward-grant count, and the relevant month are stored on our server. This allows usage limits to carry over on the same device after reinstall.

(2) Purpose
To automatically extract schedule information (title, date/time, location, notes) using AI

(3) Image context field
Content you enter in the image context field is sent to the selected AI provider as-is. Please do not enter personal information (name, address, phone number, email, etc.) or confidential information. Input is limited to 500 characters.

(4) AI Services Used

The App performs AI analysis using one of the following, depending on the provider selected at build time:

  • Anthropic Claude API (https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
  • Google Gemini API (https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Our own API using Vertex AI: images and prompts are sent to our API (e.g. Google Cloud Run), which uses Google Cloud Vertex AI for analysis. Processing is subject to Google Cloud's privacy notice (https://cloud.google.com/terms/cloud-privacy-notice).

The service used depends on the App's build configuration. Supported providers may change without notice for improvements.

(5) Data Handling

  • Images are deleted from the server immediately after API analysis
  • Processing is in accordance with each AI service provider's privacy policy
  • The App is configured so that user data is not used for AI training purposes

(6) Protection of Personal Information
The App instructs the AI to extract only schedule-related fields and not to extract personally identifiable information (name, address, phone number, etc.). However, we cannot guarantee complete prevention given the nature of image content.

(7) Advertising (Google AdMob)
When you use the free plan, the App uses Google AdMob (Google LLC) to display ads.

  • Information that may be collected: device identifiers, advertising ID, IP address, app usage, etc. (as described in Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Purpose: To display and deliver ads and optimize ad delivery
  • Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • If you use the Basic or Premium plan, ads are not shown and the above data collection for ad delivery does not apply.

3. Use of Information

Collected information is used only for:

  • Extracting schedule information from images
  • Adding extracted events to the calendar
  • Improving the App and fixing issues

When using the free plan, device information etc. may be collected by Google AdMob for ad delivery and optimization. See "Third-Party Services" â€“ Advertising above.

The App does not use information for:

  • Tracking user behavior (except for ad optimization)
  • Selling or providing data to third parties (except as required for AI analysis API and advertising services)

4. Data Retention

  • Image data: Deleted immediately after analysis (retention: seconds)
  • Extracted schedule data: Deleted from the App after calendar registration
  • Events added to the calendar: Stored in your device's calendar app until you delete them

The App does not store user data on any server, except that for free-plan usage management we store usage count, reward-grant count, and the relevant month linked to a device identifier on our server.

5. Data Sharing

The App does not share your information with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • When you have given consent
  • As described in "Third-Party Services" (AI analysis API use)

6. Security

The App protects your information by:

  • Keeping images only temporarily (no permanent storage)
  • Using encrypted API communication (HTTPS)
  • Minimizing data kept in the App
  • Configuring AI services so that user data is not used for training

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke camera, photo, or calendar access (via Android Settings)
  • Uninstall the App (all App data will be removed)
  • Delete events added to the calendar (via your device's calendar app)

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. If a child under 13 uses the App, they should do so with parental consent and supervision.
